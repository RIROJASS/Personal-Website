Andean Lithium Deposit Research

Ongoing research with mentor Martin Molan focused on mapping lithium deposits along the northern edge of the Andean Mountain Range using remote sensing and machine learning.

Research Objectives:

  • Identify geological signatures of lithium salt deposits using multispectral satellite imagery
  • Apply machine learning algorithms to detect potential new lithium sources
  • Analyze environmental impact of existing lithium extraction operations
  • Map the relationship between indigenous communities and lithium resource areas
  • Building upon my previous work with the Tepui Analysis Project

This interdisciplinary research combines geological mapping, environmental analysis, and cultural considerations of resource extraction in indigenous territories.
