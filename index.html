Multimodal AI Internship Completion

Recently wrapped up my internship focused on multimodal AI applications, where I helped develop systems that can process and analyze multiple types of data simultaneously.

I had the opportunity to work on embedding & analyzing visual data for archaeological artifact classification, creating AI that makes research easier.

Florida Undergraduate Research Conference 2025

For Valentine's day this year, my date was my first researh poster. I presented "Finding Lost Archaeological Sites with AI: Object Detection with Remote Sensing" at the 2025 Florida Undergraduate Research Conference alongside lab partner Olivia Zhang.

My presentation showcased how machine learning algorithms can identify potential archaeological sites from satellite imagery with more efficiency than traditional methods. I had a blast looking at everyone's posters and left chock-full of ideas for future investigation!
